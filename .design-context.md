# Design Context — Globe CRM

## Service Languages
- English only

## Target Audience
- B2C — general individuals managing personal/professional networks
- Age range: 25-45, tech-comfortable but not necessarily developers
- Use case: personal CRM, contact management, relationship tracking with geographic visualization

## Brand Personality
- **Warm Professional** — organized and trustworthy, but with warmth that makes personal networking feel approachable
- Not cold corporate, not playful — the sweet spot between "reliable tool" and "personal companion"

## Aesthetic Direction
- **Terrazzo** — warm textures, organic feeling, earthy tones
- Anti-AI-slop: no purple gradients, no glassmorphism stacking, no neon accents, no bounce easing
- Warm neutrals (stone family) over cool grays
- Coral accent for energy and humanity

## Color Strategy
- Primary: Charcoal (#292524) — warm dark, stone-800
- Secondary: Warm Gray (#a8a29e) — stone-400
- Accent: Coral (#f97316) — orange-500, CTAs and active states
- Background: Cream (#fffbf5) — warm white, not pure white
- Destructive: Ember Red (#dc2626)
- Success: Forest Green (#16a34a)
- Dark mode: Inverted warm palette, not cold blue-black

## Typography
- Primary: Geist Sans (latin subsets only)
- Monospace: Geist Mono (code, data displays)
- No CJK fonts needed

## Component Library
- Web: shadcn/ui (base-nova) + Tailwind v4
- Mobile: Forui + Material 3
- Icons: Lucide React (web), Forui Icons (mobile)

## Accessibility
- WCAG AA minimum
- prefers-reduced-motion respected
- Focus indicators on all interactive elements
- Minimum touch target: 44x44pt on mobile

## Reference Aesthetic
- Clay (personal CRM) — warm, relationship-focused
- Linear — clean data density, excellent spacing
- Notion — breathable whitespace, editorial feel
- Bear (notes app) — warm tones, organic feel
